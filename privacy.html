YOUR PRIVACY MATTERS
Privacy Policy
Last updated: January 2026

At Little Light Storybooks, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the personal information you provide when ordering a personalized storybook.

1

Information We Collect

When you place an order, we collect the following information:

  • Child's name and uploaded photo
  • Dedication message and giver's name
  • Buyer's full name, contact number, and delivery address
  • Payment screenshot (GCash transaction proof)
  • Device and browser information (collected automatically by Meta Pixel for analytics)
2

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To create your personalized storybook
  • To verify your payment
  • To process and deliver your order
  • To contact you regarding your order status
  • To improve our service through anonymized analytics (Meta Pixel)
3

How We Store Your Information

Your order data and uploaded images are stored securely on our servers. Your child's photo and order information are retained for a maximum of 1 week after your order is placed. Once your book is delivered and the retention period ends, your personal files are removed from our system.

We use Cloudinary for secure image storage and industry-standard encryption for all data in transit.

4

Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party services to operate our platform:

  • Cloudinary — secure image storage and delivery
  • Meta Pixel & Conversions API — anonymous marketing analytics to improve our ads
  • GCash — payment is processed directly by you through the GCash app; we do not store your GCash credentials
  • Courier partners — your delivery address is shared with our courier for order fulfillment only
5

We Do Not Sell Your Data

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third party for marketing or commercial purposes. Your data is used exclusively for fulfilling your order.

6

Your Child's Photo

Your child's photo is used solely to create the personalized storybook you ordered. It is never shared, published, or used for any other purpose. We treat every photo with the utmost care and respect.

7

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent at any time by contacting us

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us through our Facebook page or via Messenger.

8

Cookies & Tracking

We use Meta Pixel to track anonymous purchase events for advertising optimization. This helps us show our books to people who may be interested. No personally identifiable information is shared with Meta through this process.

9

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us:

  • Facebook: facebook.com/littlelightstorybooks
  • Instagram: instagram.com/littlelightstorybooks
  • Location: Quezon City, Philippines

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
